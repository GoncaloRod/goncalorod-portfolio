Pac-Man Wars

Group Project Monogame C#

Pac-Man Wars is a local multiplayer game based on the original Pac-Man. The players play against each other and the goal is to defeat the other player by not running out lives sooner than him.

This project was made on my first year of my degree together with a friend.

At that time we didn't have much knowledge about game AI so that part turned out to be the achilles heel of this game.

Controls

  • W A S D - Player 1 Movement
  • Up Left Down Right - Player 2 Movement
