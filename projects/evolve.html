Evolve

Group Project Unity C#

Evolve is a 3D platform game that takes the player through the evolution of species, from the time in which dinosaurs roam the earth up until the first human comes to exist.

The game also comes with a companion app where you can explore the game character in Augmented Reality.

This project was developed for a game development course of the second year of my degree together with a friend.

I was responsible for implementing all the mechanics and game systems as well as modelling some assets, like the main characters and the wooden crates.

I was also the one responsible for the Augmented Reality companion app.

Controls

  • W A S D / Left Stick / D-Pad - Movement
  • Space / Gamepad South Button - Jump
  • Q / Gamepad West Button - Ground Pound
  • Mouse / Right Stick - Camera Rotation
